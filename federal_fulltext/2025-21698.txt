NATIONAL ARCHIVES AND RECORDS ADMINISTRATION

Office of Government Information Services

[NARA-2026-001]


Notice of Meeting; Chief Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) 
Officers Council

AGENCY: Office of Government Information Services (OGIS), National 
Archives and Records Administration (NARA) and Office of Information 
Policy (OIP), U.S. Department of Justice (DOJ).

ACTION: Notice of meeting.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY: We are announcing a meeting of the Chief Freedom of 
Information Act (FOIA) Officers Council, co-chaired by the Director of 
OGIS and the Director of OIP.

DATES: The meeting will be on Monday, December 15, 2025, from 10:00 
a.m. to 11:30 a.m. ET. You must register to attend. (See registration 
information below.)

ADDRESSES: This meeting will be a virtual meeting. We will send access 
instructions for the meeting to those who register according to the 
instructions below.

FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Martha Murphy, by email at 
<a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#a0cfc7c9d3e0cec1d2c18ec7cfd6"><span class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="86e9e1eff5c6e8e7f4e7a8e1e9f0">[email&#160;protected]</span></a> with the subject line ``Chief FOIA Officers Council,'' or 
by telephone at 202-741-5770.

SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: This meeting is open to the public in 
accordance with the Freedom of Information Act (5 U.S.C. 552(k)). 
Additional details about the meeting, including the agenda, will be 
available on the Chief FOIA Officers Council website at <a href="https://www.foia.gov/chief-foia-officers-council">https://www.foia.gov/chief-foia-officers-council</a>.
    Procedures: The virtual meeting is open to the public. If you wish 
to offer oral public comments during the public comments periods of the 
meeting, you must register in advance at <a href="https://www.zoomgov.com/webinar/register/WN_M3CZHun6Ry6vUGzOywhbrQ">https://www.zoomgov.com/webinar/register/WN_M3CZHun6Ry6vUGzOywhbrQ</a>. You will be provided with 
information to access the meeting online. Public comments will be 
limited to three minutes per individual. We will also live-stream the 
meeting on the National Archives YouTube channel, <a href="https://youtube.com/live/VyzQWo6_0mw?feature=share">https://youtube.com/live/VyzQWo6_0mw?feature=share</a>, and include a captioning option. To 
request additional accommodations (e.g., a transcript), email 
<a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#2f4048465c6f414e5d4e01484059"><span class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="d0bfb7b9a390beb1a2b1feb7bfa6">[email&#160;protected]</span></a> or call 202-741-5770. Members of the media who wish to 
register, those who are unable to register online, and those who 
require special accommodations, should contact Martha Murphy (contact 
information listed above).

    Dated: November 24, 2025.
Alina M. Semo,
Director, Office of Government Information Services.
