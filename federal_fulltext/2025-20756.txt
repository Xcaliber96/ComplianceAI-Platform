[Docket No. VA-2025-VACO-0002]


Corporate Senior Executive Management Office Notice of 
Performance Review Board Members

AGENCY: Corporate Senior Executive Management Office, Department of 
Veterans Affairs.

ACTION: Notice.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY: Agencies are required to publish a notice in the Federal 
Register of the appointment of Performance Review Board (PRB) members. 
This notice announces the appointment of individuals to serve on the 
PRB of the Department of Veterans Affairs.

DATES:  This appointment is effective November 24, 2025.

FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Shana Love Holmon, Executive Director, 
Corporate Senior Executive Management Office, Office of Human Resources 
and Administration, 202-632-5285.
