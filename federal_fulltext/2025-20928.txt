DEPARTMENT OF ENERGY

Federal Energy Regulatory Commission

[Project No. 2210-272]


Appalachian Power Company; Notice of Application for Recreation 
Management Plan Amendment Accepted for Filing, Soliciting Comments, 
Motions To Intervene, and Protests

    Take notice that the following hydroelectric application has been 
filed with the Commission and is available for public inspection:

    a. Application Type: Recreation Management Plan Amendment.
    b. Project No: 2210-272.
    c. Date Filed: May 16, 2025, and supplemented August 7, 2025.
    d. Applicant: Appalachian Power Company.
    e. Name of Project: Smith Mountain Hydroelectric Project.
    f. Location: The project is located on the Roanoke River in 
Bedford, Campbell, Franklin, Pittsylvania, and Roanoke counties, 
Virginia. The project does not occupy federal lands. The location of 
the Leesville Tailrace Fishing Platform is in Campbell County, 
Virginia.
    g. Filed Pursuant to: Federal Power Act, 16 U.S.C. 791a-825r.
    h. Applicant Contact: Nicholas C. Sink, Appalachian Power Company, 
P.O. Box 2021, Roanoke, VA 24022-2121, (540) 985-2506, <a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#751b16061c1b1e351410055b161a18"><span class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="fc929f8f959297bc9d998cd29f9391">[email&#160;protected]</span></a>.
    i. FERC Contact: Mary Karwoski, (678) 245-3027, 
<a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#127f73606b3c797360657d61797b52747760713c757d64"><span class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="056864777c2b6e6477726a766e6c45636077662b626a73">[email&#160;protected]</span></a>.

inviting federal, state, local, and Tribal agencies with jurisdiction

should note the Commission's policy that agencies that cooperate in the 
preparation of any environmental document cannot also intervene. See 94 
FERC ] 61,076 (2001).

protests: December 5, 2025, 5:00 p.m. Eastern Time.

intervenors filing documents with the Commission to serve a copy of 
that document on each person whose name appears on the official service 
list for the project. Further, if an intervenor files comments or 
documents with the Commission relating to the merits of an issue that 
may affect the responsibilities of a particular resource agency, they 
must also serve a copy of the document on that resource agency.
    l. Description of Request: Appalachian Power Company (licensee) 
requests Commission approval to remove the requirement for the 
Leesville Tailrace Fishing Platform from the Commission approved 
Recreation Management Plan and Article 410 of the license, until such 
time that the Virginia Department of Wildlife Resources obtains 
appropriate funding and provides engineering plans for consideration by 
Appalachian Power Company. The Leesville Fishing Platform is a license 
required-recreation area which was closed in 2019 due to structural 
safety issues and portion of the platform was removed due to structural 
and public safety concerns.

    o. Comments, Protests, or Motions to Intervene: Anyone may submit

requirements of Rules of Practice and Procedure, 18 CFR 385.210, .211,

    p. Filing and Service of Documents: Any filing must (1) bear in all 
capital letters the title ``COMMENTS'', ``PROTEST'', or ``MOTION TO 
INTERVENE'' as applicable; (2) set forth in the heading the name of the 
applicant and the project number of the application to which the filing 
responds; (3) furnish the name, address, and telephone number of the 
person commenting, protesting or intervening; and (4) otherwise comply 
with the requirements of 18 CFR 385.2001 through 385.2005. All 
comments, motions to intervene, or protests must set forth their 
evidentiary basis. Any filing made by an intervenor must be accompanied 
by proof of service on all persons listed in the service list prepared 
by the Commission in this proceeding, in accordance with 18 CFR 
385.2010.
